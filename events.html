EVENTS

From intimate beachfront ceremonies to grand oceanfront galas, Mahiré Bay transforms every occasion into an unforgettable celebration. Whether you're planning a destination wedding, a corporate retreat, or a private gathering under the stars, our resort offers breathtaking venues, personalized service, and tropical elegance at every turn. With the gentle rhythm of the waves and the golden hues of sunset as your backdrop, every event at Mahiré Bay is infused with warmth, beauty, and effortless sophistication. Let us craft moments that linger long after the last toast.

DESTINATION WEDDING

Turn your dream wedding into a breathtaking reality at Mahiré Bay. Exchange vows at our iconic floating mandap, surrounded by serene waters and tropical elegance — where every moment feels like a fairytale. From decor to dining, every detail is curated with care. Begin your forever in a setting made for love.

ENGAGEMENT CEREMONY

Begin your forever in a setting as meaningful as your love. Our elegant event spaces offer the perfect ambiance for an intimate engagement filled with joy, tradition, and stunning scenery — setting the tone for a beautiful journey ahead.

HALDI • MEHENDI • SANGEET

Celebrate vibrant traditions in beautifully designed spaces. Whether it’s the colorful Mehendi, rhythmic Sangeet, or sacred Haldi, each ritual is elevated by natural surroundings, luxurious service, and unforgettable energy.

RECEPTION

Host an elegant and grand reception at Mahiré Bay — where celebration meets sophistication. From stunning decor to gourmet dining, we ensure every detail reflects your story. Dance, dine, and create memories that last a lifetime.

CORPORATE MEETINGS

Inspire productivity in a tranquil, distraction-free environment. Our professional spaces are fully equipped for board meetings, conferences, and creative sessions — enhanced by the calming presence of nature and refined hospitality.

COMPANY ANNUAL EVENTS

Reimagine your annual gatherings with a perfect blend of luxury and energy. From team awards to executive retreats, we offer seamless planning, elegant venues, and curated experiences that leave lasting impressions on your team.

BIRTHDAY PARTIES

Make every birthday a memory to cherish — whether it’s an intimate lakeside dinner or a themed celebration with loved ones. With personalized decor, curated menus, and magical surroundings, your special day becomes truly unforgettable.

BABY SHOWER

Celebrate the joy of new beginnings in a serene and heartwarming setting. Our beautifully styled spaces and gentle ambiance provide the perfect atmosphere for a meaningful baby shower surrounded by love, laughter, and blessings.

CANDLELIGHT DINNERS

Experience love in its purest form with a lakeside candlelight dinner under the stars. Surrounded by serenity and soft candlelight, every moment becomes more intimate — the perfect setting for romance and connection.
