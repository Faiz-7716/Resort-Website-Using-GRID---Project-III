ROOMS & SUITES

Experience barefoot luxury at Mahiré Bay, where every room and suite is thoughtfully designed to reflect the soul of the sea. Whether you're waking up to sun-kissed ocean views or retreating into a cocoon of calm beside the palms, our accommodations blend modern comfort with natural beauty. Each stay offers elegant interiors, private decks, and seamless indoor-outdoor living, crafted for travelers seeking tranquility, indulgence, and unforgettable moments by the beach. Nestled along the serene coastline of Mahiré Bay, our Rooms & Suites are a harmonious blend of contemporary elegance and natural charm. Whether perched above gentle waves or tucked within tropical greenery, each space invites you to experience true beachfront indulgence.
